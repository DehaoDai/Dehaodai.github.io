Textbook

Time Series Analysis and Its Applications by Shumway, Robert H. & Stoffer, David S, Vol. 4 New York: springer, 2017

Structure of the Course

Analysis of trends and seasonal effects, autoregressive and moving averages models, forecasting, informal introduction to spectral analysis.
